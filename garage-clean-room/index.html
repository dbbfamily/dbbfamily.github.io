Water Management

Diagram

      

        
Water enters via the tilt door on the left and flows right across the slab. The side door at the bottom-right corner is your natural drainage exit to the backyard.

        

          
The raised subfloor creates a 40–50mm channel between the slab and your living surface.

          
Timber bearers run front-to-back (top-to-bottom on plan) so water flows freely between them left→right toward the side door.

          
The side door is deliberately outside the enclosure — between the plastic wall and gyprock wall — serving as the drainage exit.

          
A 10–20mm gap between subfloor edges and walls prevents pooling.

          
After heavy rain, check under the subfloor. If water isn’t draining, shim bearers to encourage flow toward the side door.
