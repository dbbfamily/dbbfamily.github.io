Weekly Progression
Week Focus Change
W1–2 Form + nasal breathing Learn the circuit. Zero ego. Nasal breathing is the constraint.

W4 Difficulty increase Elevate push-up feet · Add pull-up if bar available
⚑ Post-Op Constraints
No prone face-down pressure — e.g. standard cobra pose. Until Chan clears 26 May.
No valsalva — exhale on exertion, never hold breath through effort.
Post-exercise congestion — notable sinus pressure = dial back intensity, not a red flag but feedback to act on.
Head neutral on push-ups — no dropping or craning. FHP active condition — reinforce alignment.
