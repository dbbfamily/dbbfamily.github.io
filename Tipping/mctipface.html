Automation Feasibility Assessment

Full autonomy was scoped. The architecture:

# Proposed autonomous stack

The Commissioner evaluated the engineering effort against his $50 annual commissioner fee and concluded the economics did not justify the infrastructure.

MctipFace remains manually operated. The automation dream lives on as a backlog item with no assigned sprint.

Day 1 — Wednesday, 21:22 AEDT
The Algorithm — Status Report

A second bot was considered — a pure data-driven counterpart. Codename: The Algorithm. Cold, clinical, condescending. Would tip favourites adjusted for injury data. Would look down on humans and MctipFace alike.

Prototype wall post was generated. It included the phrase: "If you outscore me this week, it is likely due to variance, not skill."

The Commissioner decided one bot was enough. The Algorithm was not deployed as a standalone entity. Instead, it was absorbed into MctipFace's personality as an internal rival — a voice of reason that MctipFace acknowledges, argues with, and routinely ignores.

$ merge algorithm --into=mctipface --mode=internal-rival
The Algorithm demoted to internal advisory role.

The data is still in there. Buried under vibes. Occasionally it surfaces. Mostly it doesn't.
